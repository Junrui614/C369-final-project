How it works?

When a transaction account is created, the relevant information is stored on the blockchain.

When you enter the account number, ID, or QR code into this site, the information will be compared with what was stored on the blockchain.

We'll check the information and tell you whether it's from the place that the account is aligned with.

You can compare and see if it matches the place you want to transfer money to.
